# Resources

## Job Boards & Aggregators

- [LinkedIn Jobs](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/) — the largest professional job board
- [Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/) — widest coverage across industries
- [Glassdoor](https://www.glassdoor.com/) — job listings with salary and interview data
- [Wellfound](https://wellfound.com/) — startup-focused jobs (formerly AngelList Talent)
- [Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/jobs) — tech jobs with verified compensation data
- [Otta](https://otta.com/) — curated tech roles with company insights
- [Hired](https://hired.com/) — companies apply to you based on your profile

## Interview Preparation

### Coding Practice
- [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/) — the standard for coding interview prep
- [NeetCode](https://neetcode.io/) — curated problem sets organized by pattern
- [Grind 75](https://www.techinterviewhandbook.org/grind75) — optimized study plan for coding interviews
- [AlgoExpert](https://www.algoexpert.io/) — video explanations for 200+ problems

### System Design
- [System Design Primer](https://github.com/donnemartin/system-design-primer) — comprehensive open-source guide
- [ByteByteGo](https://bytebytego.com/) — visual system design explanations
- [Designing Data-Intensive Applications](https://dataintensive.net/) — the definitive book on distributed systems

### Behavioral
- [Tech Interview Handbook](https://www.techinterviewhandbook.org/) — free, comprehensive guide
- [STAR Method Guide](https://www.themuse.com/advice/star-interview-method) — structuring behavioral answers

## Salary & Compensation

- [Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/) — verified tech compensation data
- [Glassdoor Salaries](https://www.glassdoor.com/Salaries/) — salary reports by company and role
- [Blind](https://www.teamblind.com/) — anonymous professional network with comp discussions
- [H1B Salary Database](https://h1bdata.info/) — public H1B visa salary data

## Visa & Immigration

- [H1B Grader](https://www.h1bgrader.com/) — check a company's H1B approval rate
- [MyVisaJobs](https://www.myvisajobs.com/) — track employer visa sponsorship history
- [ImmiHelp](https://www.immihelp.com/) — visa process guides and forums
