# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Job Hunt Engine a welcoming experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity,
experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Examples of positive behavior**

- Being respectful in issues, PRs, and reviews
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for job seekers and the project
- Crediting upstream sources and human curators

**Unacceptable behavior**

- Harassment, trolling, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others’ private information (including curator passphrases, cookies, tokens)
- Spam job submissions or malicious links
- Impersonating employers or curators

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately via the channel in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) or by
contacting the maintainer [@EshwarCVS](https://github.com/EshwarCVS).

Maintainers may remove content, block accounts, or reject contributions that
violate this code.

## Scope

This Code applies in project spaces (GitHub issues, PRs, Discussions) and when
someone is representing the project.
